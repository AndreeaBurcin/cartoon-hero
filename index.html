The Powerpuff Girls

About the Powerpuff girls

The Powerpuff Girls Blossom, Bubbles, and Buttercup are three kindergarten-aged girls with superpowers. They all live in the fictional city of Townsville with their father an creator, a scientist names Professor Utonium. The girls are frequently called upon by the city's mayor to help fight nearby criminals and other enemies using their powers.
